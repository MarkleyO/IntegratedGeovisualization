Over the last decade, hip-hop has taken the place of the most popular genre in the United States. Data on this chart and the following are taken from the Billboard Top 100 Song charts for each year. The popularity of hip-hop continues to rise into 2019. For this chart, I counted songs with features from hip-hop atists as hip-hop songs.

In 2019, Atlanta stands as the undisputed hip-hop capitol of the world. Not only does Atlanta have many of the most popular and highest charting rappers, but it has had a strong underground scene for the last 25 years. Many of the music industry's most desired producers also come from and reside in Atlanta. Examples include Atlanta natives Metro Boomin, Pierre Bourne, Mike-Will-Made-It, Sonny Digital, and many more.
